# policy_levers

Identify and prioritize outreach to U.S. policy makers (Congress, federal agencies, regulatory bodies) on AI economic impact and safety issues.

See `CLAUDE.md` for project scope, structure, and conventions.

## Research materials

- `papers/` — source literature PDFs (indexed in `PAPER_INDEX.md`, summarized in `PAPER_SUMMARIES.md`)
- `papers/text/` — extracted text
- `docs/active/<branch>/` — active research lines (convos, plans, results)
- `docs/historical/` — archived research lines (see `STATUS.md` for the index)
