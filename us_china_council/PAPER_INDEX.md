## Center of gravity

For AI/tech policy work, **Chapter 3 (Emerging Technologies)** is the central chapter — it is where USCC's framing of PRC AI risk and U.S. countermeasures lives, and is the chapter most likely to be cited in subsequent congressional and executive-branch documents. The **Recommendations** document (file 01) is the second-highest-leverage extract: it is what USCC actually asks Congress to do, in compact form. For competitive-economic policy framing, **Chapter 6** is also important. Chapters 5, 7, 8, 9, 10 are regionally or topically focused and are reference material rather than primary inputs for AI policy.
