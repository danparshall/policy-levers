## The Crisis (as of Feb 27, 2026)
Pentagon (SecDef Hegseth) gave Anthropic an ultimatum: remove all AI safety restrictions by **Friday Feb 27, 5:01 PM** or face (1) supply chain risk designation and/or (2) Defense Production Act invocation. 

**Anthropic's two redlines:** No mass surveillance of Americans. No autonomous lethal weapons without human judgment.

**Key facts:**
- Anthropic has a $200M DoD contract that originally included these restrictions
- Anthropic is the first AI company actually deployed on classified networks (xAI agreed Monday but not yet deployed)
- DoD Directive 3000.09 requires "appropriate levels of human judgment" (NOT strict human-in-the-loop — important nuance)
- Wednesday night: Pentagon sent "best and final offer"
- Coalition letter to Congress urging hearings already circulating
- Sen. Van Hollen already tweeted criticism
