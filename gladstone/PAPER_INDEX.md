## Center of gravity

LOE4 is the longest substantive chapter (38 pp) and the document's intellectual core — the operational annexes (I, K, N, O, P, Q) are almost entirely there to flesh out FAISA's licensing tiers. LOE1 reads as scaffolding to buy time for LOE4 to be enacted; LOE5 extends LOE4 multilaterally. If you only have time to read one chapter, read LOE4.
